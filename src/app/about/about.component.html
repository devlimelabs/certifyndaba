Our Story

We have extensive experience in connecting Individuals & Employers in the ABA space. Having spoken to thousands of professionals in ABA over the past decade, the need to protect the individual’s confidentiality and time became the imperative when developing this community. Individuals want to be aware of what’s happening in their industry and at the same time be able to choose where they engage. In addition, due to the overwhelming shortage of individuals in ABA, we knew that a brighter light needed to be shined on it pointing people to it as a career option. Finally, employers are looking for an efficient and cost-effective solution to connect with ABA professionals. CertifyndABA empowers individuals to connect with confidence allowing you to be in control of your career.

Our Mission

Our mission at CertifyndABA is to bring visibility to this community and simplify how clinics and individuals connect with each other to serve those in need.

As we strive to revolutionize the way professionals and organizations connect, our mission goes beyond facilitating career opportunities. We aim to create a supportive community that empowers individuals to continuously develop their skills, expand their knowledge, and stay current with industry trends. By offering resources, insights, and a platform for collaboration, we are dedicated to fostering the professional growth of our users and contributing to the overall advancement of their respective fields. In doing so, we hope to inspire a new generation of talent and drive meaningful change within the industry.
