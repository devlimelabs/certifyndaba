The CertifyndABA Process

How It Works

Discover the CertifyndABA Process, where ABA professionals take the lead. Our tailored steps place you at the forefront, inviting the right opportunities directly to your door.

1

Individual Verification

Complete your profile, enter your certification, experience, and job preferences. We manually verify all BACB credentials for a trusted network.

2

Confidential Matching

Employers search based on qualifications and only see your professional info. You stay anonymous until you choose to accept a request.

3

Connection Requests

Receive connection requests direct from hiring managers, complete with job details and salary upfront. You decide which employers to engage with.

4

Make Your Choice

Accept to share your contact details and proceed to an interview, or reject to stay hidden from that employer for 30 days.

5

Control Your Career

Enjoy the power to manage your career path. Let employers apply to you, maintaining your privacy until you're ready.
